User Balance

Earn UWU Money by messaging in servers with UwU Bot. Add the bot to your server to boost activity and let users spend their earnings in various ways.

Go hunting to find animals, items, and lootboxes. Send animals to your barn and open lootboxes for rare rewards. A small fee applies for each hunt.

Lottery Draw

Buy lottery cards from the lottery shop and try your luck. Match the drawn numbers to win UWU cash prizes. An exciting way to spend and potentially earn more.
