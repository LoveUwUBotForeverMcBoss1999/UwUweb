About UwU Bot

UwU Bot is a fun and entertaining bot created by Miyuru Gunatilake (McBoss), a solo developer who codes in Python, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. This bot is designed to bring all sorts of fun stuff to your Discord server.

The Developer

Miyuru Gunatilake (McBoss) is doing this all alone, without any staff or team. He is the sole developer behind UwU Bot, working tirelessly to maintain and improve the bot's functionality.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide Discord users with a fun, engaging, and versatile bot that enhances their server experience. We strive to continually update and improve UwU Bot to meet the evolving needs of our users.
