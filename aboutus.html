About UwU Bot

Overview

UwU Bot is designed to keep Discord servers active and engage members, created by Miyuru Gunatilake (McBoss). The bot focuses on economy features to enhance server interaction.

Miyuru Gunatilake (McBoss) is the sole developer behind UwU Bot. Building and maintaining the bot is a challenging yet rewarding experience.
