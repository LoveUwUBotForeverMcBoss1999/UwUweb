Economy Commands

uwu balance - Display your balance

uwu balance {@user} - Display others balance

uwu buy - Buy items from the shop

uwu shop - Visit the shop

uwu pay {@user} - Pay/Transfer UWU to members/users

Bank Commands

uwu bsetup - Setup a bank account for banking

uwu bprofile - Get the bank account info and to see the bank account profile

uwu bbalance - To get your current bank balance

uwu bdelete - Delete your current bank account

uwu bwithdraw [withdraw money amount] - Withdraw UWU Money, Bank to Balance

uwu bdeposit [deposit money amount] - Deposit UWU Money into the bank

uwu bloan [amount] - Get a bank loan to your account

uwu bloansettle [amount you want to settle] - Settle a loan amount you want

uwu bloaninfo - Get your current bank loan status/info

Animals Commands

uwu hunt - Go hunt on items, animals, lootboxes

uwu inv - Display your inventory

uwu send_to_barn - Send the all animals in your inventory to barn

uwu barn - Display your barn

uwu lootbox - Open lootboxes

Gamble Commands

uwu roll [dice sides] [bet amount] - Roll a dice and bet an amount

uwu coinflip [bet] [heads or tails] - Place the bids and flip the coin

uwu lottery_buy [ticket number] - To buy Lottery Card

uwu lottery_shop - Display lottery shop
