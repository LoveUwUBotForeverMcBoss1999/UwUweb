Frequently Asked Questions

What is UwU Bot?

UwU Bot is a fun and entertaining Discord bot created by a solo developer. It brings various features and commands to enhance your Discord server experience.

How do I invite UwU Bot to my server?

You can invite UwU Bot to your server by clicking the "Invite" link in the navigation menu or by using the official invite link provided on our Discord server.

Is UwU Bot free to use?

Yes, UwU Bot is free to use! However, we appreciate donations to help cover server costs and support ongoing development. It's FREE for everyone. Invite UwU Now!

How often is UwU Bot updated?

UwU Bot is regularly updated with new features, improvements, and bug fixes. Check our Discord server or the "Status" section for the latest updates.

How can I report a bug or suggest a feature?

You can report bugs or suggest features by joining our official Discord server and using the appropriate channels. We appreciate your feedback! You can use the "uwu suggest" command to suggest enything to the UwU Bot.
